Points

Number of points is diversed and it depends of game settings. But with different settings one thing doesnt change - you get points for strikes. Table below describes the points for each striked ball:

    5 colors - 1 point
    7 colors - 2 points
    9 colors - 4 points

When option "Show next colors" is turned on the total amount of points is decreased for 1/3 - showing you a hint makes game easier so you get less points.
